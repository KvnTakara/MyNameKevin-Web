Chris Pine as

Edgin, The Bard

Edgin Darvis, a bard and former member of the Harpers. After his wife was murdered, he raised his daughter Kira with his friend Holga. In prison with Holga following a heist gone wrong, he plots their escape in hopes of being reunited with his daughter.

Michelle Rodriguez as

Holga, The Barbarian

Holga Kilgore, a barbarian who was exiled from the Uthgardt Elk Tribe for marrying an outsider. She acts as a surrogate mother for Kira and is imprisoned with Edgin.

Regé-Jean Page as

Xenk, The Paladin

Xenk Yendar, a paladin who narrowly escaped the lich Szass Tam's "rise to power" in Thay and as a result, "ages more slowly than a normal human".

Sophia Lillis as

Doric, The Druid

Doric, a tiefling druid raised in the Neverwinter Wood by a wood elf enclave. She is a member of the Emerald Enclave and has organized a resistance against the Lord of Neverwinter who targeted the forest "for its resources".

Hugh Grant as

Forge, The Rogue

Forge Fitzwilliam, an ambitious rogue. He is a former member of Edgin's crew and has been taking care of Kira. Since Edgin's imprisonment, he has become the Lord of Neverwinter and gained great wealth.

Justice Smith as

Simon, The Sorcerer

Simon Aumar, a half-elf wild magic sorcerer who is the descendant of Elminster Aumar, a notable wizard.

Daisy Head as

Sofina, The Wizard

Sofina, a Red Wizard of Thay with a focus in necromancy and "ties to Thay's tyrannical magocracy".
